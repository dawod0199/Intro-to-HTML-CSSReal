Travel to St. John's Newfoundland!

St. John's, the capital of Newfoundland, is the perfect destination for your next vacation. Whether travelling with your family, friends, or exploring by yourself, St. John's has endless treasures to explore and is filled with a vibrant culture.

Still not convinced? Here are some of the numerous reasons why St. John's is an amazing travel destination:

  • The scenery is breathtaking due to the colourful jellybean homes, ocean views and mountains
  • It is filled with rich history and traditions
  • There are various types of activities for you to do during your stay
  • You can go and find loads of things to do all year round, regardless of the season

Must do's:

  1. Go to Signal Hill
  2. Visit the Cape Spear Lighthouse
  3. Eat the famous Fish n' Chips at Ches's
  4. Go on a boat tour to whale watch and see some puffins
  5. See the enormous icebergs
  6. Get screeched in

Hopefully, it is now clear to you where you'll be heading next. St. John's is a beautiful city with great people, food, and sceney that will make your dream vacation a reality.
